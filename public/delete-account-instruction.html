At "Link", we prioritize your digital autonomy. If you've made the decision to leave, we're here to help make the process as seamless as possible. We've published step-by-step photo guides on how to delete your account across different platforms: Web, iOS, and Android. Please note: once you initiate the account deletion, there's a 30-day grace period. If you log in within these 30 days, your account will be reactivated. However, after the grace period, your account, including all posts, comments, and associated data, will be permanently deleted.
